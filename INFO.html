WHAT IS FINØ?

areyouFINØ (better known as FINØ) is a passion project and clothing line created, edited, and directed by Riely Rufino. FINØ is not just a clothing brand — it is a multi-media creative outlet and a collection of works ranging from visual arts, graphic design, photography and film, music production, and fashion. Utilizing one-of-one vintage garments to create unique pieces, FINØ has presented itself with its exclusivity; it reflects a deep commitment to intentional design, attracting an audience that not only appreciates fashion but is also drawn to the layered storytelling and creative process behind each work. FINØ can be anything, and will be anything for the future. FINØ is for the people.
